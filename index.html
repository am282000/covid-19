India is approaching the ninth month of the coronavirus pandemic with more than five million confirmed cases - the second-highest in the world
after the US - and more than 80,000 reported deaths. Seven states are worst affected - accounting for about 48% of India's population.

Scientists and Doctors are working on vaccine. Till the time you don't get vaccine, we have to take care of ourselves and our family. All we can do follow the guidelines provided by WHO. By maintaining social distancing, using masks, washing hands time to time, using sanitizers .
